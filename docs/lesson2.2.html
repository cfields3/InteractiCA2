Lesson 2.2 - Conway's Game of Life

Throughout this lesson, we will investigate unique patterns in Conway's game of life then, let you play with the simulation, then modify it!

Learning Objectives

By the end of Lesson 2.2, you should be able to...

  • Identify the period of a pattern
  • Learn terminology for classes of patterns
  • Create different cellular automata rulesets
  • Gain understanding of why Conway's game of life is unique
